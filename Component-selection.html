Individual Component Selection

This page documents my individual component selection including considered options for each "role" as well as the logic behind my hardware choice. Due to project requirements, through-hole components will not be considered for selection.

Major Hardware Selection

Power Regulator Selection

For my power regulator selection, I am looking to minimize the number of required support components, and simplify the circuit as much as possible. This way, debugging will be much easier because there are less areas where something could go wrong.

Component Image Advantages Disadvantages Hyperlink
ADPL44002AUJZ-3.3-R7
  • Fixed output
  • Can handle up to 40V input
  • Lower cost than other option
  • more complex circuit
  • Many support components needed
REG1117-3.3
  • Small, simple circuit
  • Only requires 2 10uF capacitors
  • fixed output
  • Only 3 connections
  • low max input voltage
  • vey small, difficult to solder

My Selection

Component Image Justification
REG1117-3.3 The main draw of this component is the simplicity. The only support components this regulator requires is 2 capacitors. It has only 3 terminals, making it a quick and simple solder job as well.
Potentiometers

The main function of this system is to read the values from 2 potentiometers, then send that information downstream to my teammate's systems. The potentiometers are also the component of the exhibit that the user will interact with. It is critical that I find a good potentiometer.

Component Image Advantages Disadvantages Hyperlink
MH22B-10-1K
  • Good size
  • Designed for panel mount
  • Dial shaft is good shape for creating a custom knob to fit over this one
  • very expensive
  • technically not surface mount
RK1191124001
  • affordable price
  • workable dial shaft
  • Small size
  • May be difficult to position inside exhibit due to it being surface mount

My Selection

Component Image Justification
RK1191124001 Surface mount potentiometers that have shafts are surprisingly difficult to find, especially at a good price. This one checks the boxes for what I need. It is surface mount, it has a shaft that can have a knob made for it, and it can connect to my microcontroller.
